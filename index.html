Technical Explanation

Our state of the art multirotor cellular phone flight system uses the best-in-class accelerometer and feather-light carbon graphene rotor technology to effectively sustain flight for up to 25 minutes. Our aftermarket solutions are battle-tested in the most rigorous environments, and we hold our manufacturers to the highest standard. Our accelerometer that detects when the phone enters free-fall is the same technology that multiple militaries teams use for their UAV and semi-autonomous drone needs. The gyroscopic stabilizer effortlessly rights the phone during free-fall, and maintains a stable heading and orientation in every scenario. We’ve assembled the best industrial designers from JPL, NASA, Apple, and IKEA, who have constructed lightweight, effective, and flexible inconel titanium alloy rotor wings that weigh less than 1.2 grams, and are sturdy enough to cut through belligerent human flesh with ease. Our 2.6 K motor is one of the most power-efficient yet powerful on the market, producing over 500 grams of thrust per propeller. The combination of the powerful motors, durable yet lightweight rotors, and expertly-made chassis mean that your Hummingbird flight system will maintain height and speed in the harshest wind, cold, or rain. The carefully-constructed carbon-fiber housing protects the internal components from sand, snow, or other particulates. .
